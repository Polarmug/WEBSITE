4Ps Partylist

General Information

Partylist Name: Pagtibayin at Palaguin Pangkabuhayan Pilipino (4Ps Partylist)

Acronym: 4Ps

Current Representative: Marcelino "Nonoy" Libanan

Year Founded/Registered: 2019

Sectoral Advocacy: Poverty alleviation, social welfare, and the empowerment of marginalized sectors.

Platforms & Legislative Focus

  • Authored and co-authored a total of 271 House Bills and filed 117 House resolutions on various issues and sectors as a member of congress

Controversies

  • Denounced for the using the name of the Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program (4Ps) Act to gain popularity in elections despite having nothing to do with the said law
