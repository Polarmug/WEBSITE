Political Platforms

Senator Pia Cayetano's legislative agenda focuses on:

  • Healthcare Reform: Advocating for accessible and quality healthcare services.
  • Education Advancement: Promoting inclusive and future-ready education systems.
  • Women's and Children's Rights: Ensuring the protection and empowerment of women and children.
  • Sustainable Development: Aligning national policies with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Economic Growth: Implementing fiscal reforms to stimulate business and investments.
  • Youth and Sports Development: Supporting programs that nurture athletic talents and youth engagement.

Senatorial Record

Legislative Accomplishments

Senator Cayetano has championed numerous laws across various sectors. Key accomplishments include:

  • Healthcare and Public Health
  • Responsible Parenthood and Reproductive Health Act (RA 10354) – Principal sponsor. This landmark law guarantees universal access to reproductive health care services, education, and supplies.
  • Sin Tax Reform Laws (RA 10351 and RA 11467) – Authored and advocated. These measures increased taxes on tobacco and alcohol to raise revenue for health programs and discourage vice, particularly among the youth.
  • Universal Health Care Act (RA 11223) – Co-authored. This law ensures that all Filipinos have equitable access to quality and affordable health services.
  • Rare Diseases Act (RA 10747) – Authored. The law provides early access to comprehensive medical care for patients with rare diseases.
  • Children’s Emergency Relief and Protection Act (RA 10821) – Co-authored. Safeguards children’s rights and welfare during disasters.
  • Immunization Act – Principal author. Mandates basic immunization for infants and young children to promote public health.
  • Education and Youth Development
  • Inclusive Education for Learners with Disabilities Act (RA 11650) – Authored and sponsored. Institutes provide inclusive education to ensure equal learning opportunities for learners with disabilities.
  • Excellence in Teacher Education Act (RA 11713) – Co-authored. Aims to modernize and professionalize the teacher education system.
  • Ladderized Education Act (RA 10647) – Authored. Integrates technical-vocational training and higher education into a flexible learning pathway.
  • Unified Student Financial Assistance System for Tertiary Education (UniFAST) Act (RA 10687) – Authored. Streamlines and integrates government scholarship and student loan programs.
  • Open Distance Learning Act (RA 10650) – Principal sponsor. Enables lifelong learning through open and distance education.
  • Open High School System Act (RA 10665) – Principal sponsor. Establishes a flexible secondary education system for students unable to attend regular high school.
  • Women’s and Children’s Rights
  • Expanded Maternity Leave Law (RA 11210) – Authored. Grants 105 days of paid maternity leave to support working mothers.
  • Expanded Breastfeeding Promotion Act (RA 10028) – Authored. Mandates workplace support for breastfeeding mothers and promotes breastfeeding in public and private institutions.
  • Foundling Recognition and Protection Act (RA 11767) – Co-authored and co-sponsored. Recognizes foundlings as natural-born Filipino citizens and secures their rights.
  • Domestic Administrative Adoption and Alternative Child Care Act (RA 11642) – Co-authored and co-sponsored. Simplifies and streamlines the adoption process and strengthens alternative child care.
  • Magna Carta of Women (RA 9710) – Advocate and sponsor. Guarantees women’s rights across multiple sectors and ensures gender equality.
  • Economic and Fiscal Reforms
  • Corporate Recovery and Tax Incentives for Enterprises (CREATE) Act (RA 11534) – Principal sponsor. Reduces corporate income tax and rationalizes fiscal incentives to support economic recovery.
  • Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators (POGO) Law (RA 11590) – Principal author. Imposes appropriate taxes on offshore gaming operations to generate government revenue.
  • Energy and Environmental Protection
  • Philippine Natural Gas Industry Development Act (RA 12120) – Sponsored. Supports the growth of the natural gas industry to diversify the country’s energy mix.
  • Environmental Awareness and Education Act (RA 9512) – Authored. Integrates environmental education into all school levels and public awareness programs.
  • Clean Air Act, Clean Water Act, and Solid Waste Management Oversight – Co-chair and chair of joint congressional oversight committees. Oversaw the effective implementation and evaluation of key environmental laws.
  • Youth and Sports Development
  • Student-Athletes Protection Act (RA 10676) – Authored and sponsored. Protects the welfare and rights of student-athletes, including their academic and athletic balance.
  • National Academy of Sports Act (RA 11470) – Principal author. Establishes a dedicated institution for nurturing young athletes through quality education and sports training.

Controversies

  • POGO Legislation: Senator Cayetano was the principal author of the POGO Law (RA 11590), which faced criticism due to concerns over offshore gaming operations' social and economic impacts.
  • Stance on Press Freedom: She withdrew her signature from a resolution supporting the renewal of ABS-CBN's franchise, leading to questions about her position on media freedom.
  • Silence on Key Issues: Critics have alleged that Cayetano has reluctance to address sensitive and high-profile issues, creates doubt about her capacity as a leader, given her unwillingness to make difficult decisions.
  • Political Dynasty Allegations: The Cayetano family's significant political presence in Taguig and national politics has drawn scrutiny over potential political dynasty practices.
