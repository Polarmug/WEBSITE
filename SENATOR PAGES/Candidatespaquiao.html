Emmanuel "Manny" Pacquiao

Personal Information

Birthdate: December 17, 1978

Age on Election Day: 46

Birthplace: Kibawe, Bukidnon

Residence: Brgy. Alabang, Muntinlupa City, Metro Manila

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Jinkee Pacquiao

Profession/Occupation: Former Senator, Boxer

Political Party: PROMDI (Partido ng Masang Pilipino)

Platforms

  • Advocacy for poverty alleviation and support for marginalized sectors
  • Improvement of the Philippine healthcare system
  • Promotes education and skills development programs to address unemployment
  • Increase government spending on infrastructure projects for economic growth

Notable Positions

  • Senator of the Philippines (2016–2022)
  • Representative of Sarangani (2010–2016)

Notable Laws and Projects

  • Republic Act No. 10742 (Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act): Sponsored this reform law to restructure the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK), focusing on improving the youth's political participation.
  • National Sports Development Act (Republic Act No. 11214): Championed the development of the sports sector in the country, aiming to boost the support for Filipino athletes.
  • Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) Initiatives: Supported reforms aimed at expanding healthcare coverage for indigent Filipinos.

Controversies

  • Criticized for his inconsistent attendance in the Senate and his focus on his boxing career during his tenure.
  • Faced backlash for his controversial remarks about same-sex marriage and LGBTQ+ issues, which generated debates regarding his stand on human rights.
