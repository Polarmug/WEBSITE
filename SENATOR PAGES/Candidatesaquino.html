Bam Aquino

Personal Information

Birthdate: May 7, 1977

Age on Election Day: 48

Birthplace: Manila

Residence: Brgy. San Jose, Concepcion, Tarlac

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Mary Fatima G. Aquino

Profession/Occupation: Businessman

Political Party: Liberal Party (LP)

Platforms

  • Advocate for innovation and technology-driven reforms to boost economic growth
  • Focus on education and skills development programs for youth
  • Supports environmental protection and climate change mitigation initiatives

Notable Positions

  • Senator of the Philippines (2013–2019)
  • Representative of Tarlac (2007–2010)

Senatorial Record

  • Authored/Co-Authored: Over 41 laws during his tenure in the senate of the Philippines.

Notable Laws and Projects

  • Republic Act No. 10644 (Go Negosyo Act): Instrumental in the creation of this law that established the Go Negosyo Centers aimed at promoting entrepreneurship among Filipinos.
  • Republic Act No. 10648 (Iskolar ng Bayan Act of 2014): Advocated for reforms to strengthen support for MSMEs in the country.
  • Magna Carta for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (Republic Act No. 10648): Advocated for reforms to strengthen support for MSMEs in the country.

Controversies

  • His tenure as senator was questioned by some due to the alleged lack of significant legislative achievements.
  • Faced political criticisms following his involvement in controversies surrounding alleged anomalies with the use of funds in his past public office roles, though he was never formally charged.
