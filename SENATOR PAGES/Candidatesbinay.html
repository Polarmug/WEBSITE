Mar-len Abigail "Abby" Binay

Personal Information

Full Name: Mar-len Abigail Sombillo Binay

Birthdate: December 12, 1975

Age on Election Day: 49

Birthplace: Quezon City

Sex: Female

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Luis Jose Angel Nakpil Campos Jr.

Profession/Occupation: Mayor

Political Party: Nationalist People's Coalition (NPC)

Platforms

  • Expand successful Makati City programs nationwide, such as free healthcare and scholarships
  • Strengthen local government units' capacity to address climate change
  • Enhance disaster preparedness and response mechanisms

Notable Positions

  • Mayor of Makati (2015–present)
  • Representative of Makati's 2nd District (2010–2013)

Notable Programs and Projects (as Mayor)

  • Makati City Health Program: Expanded free medical services for residents, including free dialysis, cancer treatment, and surgeries
  • Makati Scholarship Program: Launched initiatives for the education of underprivileged but deserving students, providing scholarships and educational support
  • Makati's Socialized Housing Program: Provided affordable housing to displaced residents and urban poor families
  • Makati's Free Bus Rides for Senior Citizens and PWDs: Implemented free transportation services for senior citizens and persons with disabilities within Makati
  • Makati City's Disaster Preparedness and Response Programs: Enhanced local government responses to natural disasters and emergencies

Controversies

  • Involved in the 2015 Makati City Hall Building II construction scandal, which allegedly involved overpricing of the building's construction under the administration of her father, former Vice President Jejomar Binay. The issue led to accusations of corruption, though she was not directly implicated.
  • Criticized by political opponents for the perceived dynastic control of the Binay family over Makati politics.
