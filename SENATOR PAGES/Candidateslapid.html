Manuel "Lito" Lapid

Personal Information

Birthdate: October 25, 1955

Age on Election Day: 69

Birthplace: Porac, Pampanga

Residence: Brgy. Poblacion, Porac, Pampanga

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Marissa Tadeo Lapid

Profession/Occupation: Public Official / Senator

Political Party: Partido ng Masang Pilipino (PMP)

Platforms

  • Promotes the welfare of farmers and agricultural workers
  • Advocates for better healthcare services, especially in rural areas
  • Supports the creation of jobs through local industries and infrastructure
  • Calls for increased government focus on infrastructure development and national security

Notable Positions

  • Senator (2004–2016, 2019–present)
  • Governor of Pampanga (1995–2004)

Notable Laws and Projects

  • Republic Act No. 10963 (Tax Reform for Acceleration and Inclusion - TRAIN Law): Advocated for this tax reform to provide support for the marginalized sectors.
  • Expanded Senior Citizens Act of 2010 (Republic Act No. 9994): Co-authored legislation that provides additional benefits for senior citizens, including discounts and access to free medical services.
  • Advocate for Farmers: Worked on legislative measures aimed at increasing agricultural support and improving the welfare of farmers in his home province of Pampanga.

Senatorial Record

  • Authored/Co-Authored: Over 16 laws during his senatorial tenureship. However, he has filed over 600 senate bills.

Controversies

  • Involved in accusations of using his popularity as an actor to further political aspirations, though no major legal cases or allegations have been filed against him.
  • Faced criticisms regarding his absence from legislative work during his early years in the Senate, with some questioning his contributions.
