Renecio “Luke” S. Espiritu Jr.

Personal Information

Full Name: Renecio Santos Espiritu Jr.

Date of Birth: October 18, 1974

Place of Birth: Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, Philippines

Age: 50

Political Party: Partido Lakas ng Masa (PLM)

Key Advocacy Areas / Platforms

  • Ending contractualization and promoting job security.
  • Implementing a living wage for workers.
  • Strengthening labor unions and workers' rights.
  • Advocating for progressive taxation and equitable economic policies.

Notable Positions

  • Labor lawyer and activist.
  • President, Bukluran ng Manggagawang Pilipino.
  • Board of Trustees Member, Freedom from Debt Coalition.
  • Former National President, Solidarity of Unions in the Philippines for Empowerment and Reforms (SUPER) Federation.
  • Member, Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP).
  • 2022 Senatorial Candidate: Ran under PLM, focusing on labor rights and social justice.
  • 2025 Senatorial Candidate: Running under PLM with continued emphasis on workers' rights and economic reforms.

Controversies

None reported.
