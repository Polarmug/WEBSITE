Senator Robin Padilla

Personal Information

Birthdate: November 23, 1969

Age on Election Day: 55

Birthplace: Daet, Camarines Norte, Philippines

Residence: Quezon City, Metro Manila

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Mariel Rodriguez-Padilla (actress/TV host)

Profession/Occupation: Actor / Senator / Political Commentator

Political Party: PDP–Laban (aligned with the UniTeam coalition)

Political Platforms

  • Advocates constitutional reform (especially federalism and parliamentary system)
  • Pushes for the legalization of medical marijuana
  • Strong supporter of Muslim rights and Mindanao autonomy
  • Promotes Philippine cultural preservation and patriotism in education

Notable Positions

  • Senator of the Philippines (2022–present)
  • Chairperson, Senate Committee on Constitutional Amendments and Revision of Codes
  • Actor, producer, and former top action star in the 1990s

Notable Laws and Projects (Contributions)

  • Principal sponsor of resolutions pushing for Constitutional Amendments (Cha-cha), specifically economic liberalization
  • Authored Senate Bill No. 2230 — proposed amendments to allow 100% foreign ownership of industries via constitutional revision
  • Advocated strongly for the Bangsamoro Organic Law (public campaign support)
  • Supporter of Senate Bill No. 230 – Medical Cannabis Compassionate Use Act (2023 version)

Controversies

  • Previously convicted for illegal possession of firearms (1994); pardoned by President Fidel V. Ramos in 1998
  • Criticized for limited legislative experience and struggles with Senate interpellations (2022–2024)
  • Received backlash for advocating hasty constitutional changes without sufficient public consultation
