Benjamin "Benhur" Abalos Jr.

Personal Information

Full Name: Benjamin de Castro Abalos Jr.

Birthdate: July 19, 1962

Age on Election Day: 62

Birthplace: Manila, Philippines

Residence: Brgy. Plainview, Mandaluyong City

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Carmelita "Menchie" Aguilar

Children: 6

Profession/Occupation: Lawyer / Politician

Political Party: Partido Federal ng Pilipinas (PFP)

Platforms

  • Advocates for amendments to the Local Government Code to enhance decentralization and empower local government units (LGUs)
  • Promotes lower electricity rates to alleviate the financial burden on Filipino households
  • Supports food security initiatives, focusing on strengthening agricultural support systems
  • Endorses disaster preparedness and urban development, aiming for resilient and sustainable communities

Notable Positions

  • Secretary of the Interior and Local Government (2022–2024)
  • Chairman, Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (2021–2022)
  • Mayor, Mandaluyong City (1998–2004; 2007–2016)
  • Representative, Lone District of Mandaluyong (2004–2007)
  • City Councilor, Mandaluyong City (1995–1998)

Notable Laws and Projects

  • As DILG Secretary, spearheaded initiatives to streamline government processes, aiming to reduce bureaucratic red tape
  • Championed urban development projects in Mandaluyong, leading to significant infrastructure improvements during his tenure as mayor
  • Advocated for transparent governance, promoting accountability and citizen participation in local government affairs

Controversies

  • Faced scrutiny over the efficacy of disaster response mechanisms during his tenure as DILG Secretary, with critics pointing to areas needing improvement
  • Encountered challenges related to urban congestion and traffic management while serving as MMDA Chairman, leading to public debates on policy effectiveness
