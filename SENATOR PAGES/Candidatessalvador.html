Platforms

  • Advocates for better opportunities for the entertainment sector

  • Strong proponent of reforms in the justice system and police modernization

Notable Positions

Primarily known for his work in entertainment and film industry, particularly in action roles in the 1980s and 1990s.

Controversies

  • In 2006, Salvador was involved in a drug-related case, which led to his arrest for drug possession. He later underwent rehabilitation and returned to the entertainment industry after serving his sentence.
