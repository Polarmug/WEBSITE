Leodegario “Ka Leody” Q. de Guzman

Personal Information

Full Name: Leodegario Quitain de Guzman

Date of Birth: July 27, 1959

Place of Birth: Naujan, Oriental Mindoro, Philippines

Age: 66

Political Party: Partido Lakas ng Masa (PLM)

Key Advocacy Areas / Platforms

  • Labor rights and protection.
  • Universal healthcare and education.
  • Progressive taxation and wealth redistribution.
  • Environmental sustainability and opposition to environmentally harmful projects.
  • Democratic governance and anti-corruption measures.

Notable Positions

  • Chairperson, Bukluran ng Manggagawang Pilipino (BMP): A prominent labor leader advocating for workers' rights.
  • 2022 Presidential Candidate: Ran under PLM, promoting a pro-worker and socialist platform.
  • 2025 Senatorial Candidate: Running under PLM with a focus on labor rights and social justice.

Advocacy Actions / Accomplishments

  • Organized workers during the 2018 labor strike at a garments factory in Laguna, leading to successful negotiations for better wages and working conditions.
  • Participated in the "Kampanya para sa Makataong Pamumuhay" (Campaign for Decent Living), advocating for a livable wage for all workers.

Controversies

None reported.
