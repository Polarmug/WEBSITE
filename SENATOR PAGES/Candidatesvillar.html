Camille Villar

Personal Information

Birthdate: January 25, 1985

Age on Election Day: 40

Birthplace: Mandaluyong City

Residence: Brgy. Talon 2, Las Piñas, Metro Manila

Sex: Female

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Erwin Flores Genuino

Profession/Occupation: Legislator

Political Party: Nacionalista Party

Platforms

  • Focus on improving education and accessibility of healthcare
  • Supports small businesses and entrepreneurs, especially in urban areas
  • Advocates for stronger family support programs, including parental leave and child welfare initiatives

Notable Positions

  • Representative of Las Piñas City (2016–present)

Notable Laws and Projects (as Representative)

  • Kalinga Para sa Kabataan Program: Promoted education and scholarships for the youth in her district.
  • Las Piñas Livelihood Program: Implemented programs aimed at supporting small local businesses and creating more job opportunities.

Controversies

  • While she has remained relatively free from major controversies, her family’s strong political influence in Las Piñas has raised questions regarding political dynasties.
