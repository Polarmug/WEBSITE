Heidi Lloce Mendoza

Personal Information

Full Name: Heidi Lloce Mendoza

Date of Birth: November 3, 1962

Place of Birth: Tayabas, Quezon, Philippines

Age: 62

Political Party: Independent

Key Advocacy Areas / Platforms

  • Good governance and anti-corruption.
  • Transparency in public service.
  • Fiscal responsibility.
  • Procurement reform.

Notable Positions

  • Commissioner, Commission on Audit (COA) (2011–2015): Mendoza served as one of the two commissioners of COA, where she conducted several audits uncovering procurement fraud and other irregularities, leading to cases being filed in court.
  • Officer-in-Charge, COA (February–March 2015): She briefly served as the OIC of COA, overseeing its operations during a transitional period.
  • Under-Secretary-General, United Nations Office of Internal Oversight Services (OIOS) (2015–2019): Appointed by UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon, Mendoza led the UN's internal oversight body, responsible for auditing, monitoring, and evaluating the organization's operations globally.

Controversies

None reported.
