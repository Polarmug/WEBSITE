Apollo Carreon Quiboloy

Personal Information

Full Name: Apollo Carreon Quiboloy

Date of Birth: April 25, 1950

Place of Birth: Dumanlas, Buhangin, Davao City, Philippines

Age: 75

Political Party: Partido Demokratiko Pilipino–Lakas ng Bayan (PDP–Laban)

Notable Positions

  • Founder and Leader, Kingdom of Jesus Christ (KJC): Established in 1985, KJC is a Restorationist church based in the Philippines. Quiboloy proclaimed himself the "Appointed Son of God" and the "Owner of the Universe."
  • Founder, Sonshine Media Network International (SMNI): A broadcasting network associated with KJC, SMNI has been used to disseminate Quiboloy's teachings and political views.

Controversies

  • Legal Investigations and Allegations:
    • Quiboloy is wanted by the FBI for alleged participation in a labor trafficking scheme involving church members in the United States.
    • He faces serious criminal charges, including sex trafficking, coercion, and fraud, both in the U.S. and the Philippines.
    • In 2024, Quiboloy surrendered to Philippine authorities after a two-week manhunt involving over 2,000 police officers
