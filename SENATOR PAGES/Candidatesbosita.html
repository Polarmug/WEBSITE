Colonel Bonifacio Laqui Bosita

Personal Information

Birthdate: November 30, 1966

Age on Election Day: 58

Birthplace: Lucena City, Quezon, Philippines

Residence: Brgy. Puting Kahoy, Silang, Cavite

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Aida Mauzar Bosita

Profession/Occupation: Retired Police Lieutenant Colonel / Motorcycle Rights Advocate / Legislator / YouTuber

Political Party: Independent

Platforms

  • Advocates for the rights and welfare of motorcycle riders and motorists
  • Promotes road safety and aims to protect motorists from abusive traffic enforcers
  • Supports the establishment of a National Traffic Enforcement and Management Center to oversee and organize traffic enforcement nationwide

Notable Positions

  • Representative, 1-Rider Partylist, House of Representatives (2022–present)
  • Retired Police Lieutenant Colonel, Philippine National Police – Highway Patrol Group
  • Founder, Riders' Safety Advocates of the Philippines (RSAP)

Notable Laws and Projects

  • As a member of the House of Representatives, serves on multiple committees, including Appropriations, Dangerous Drugs, Government Enterprises and Privatization, Health, Labor and Employment, Metro Manila Development, People's Participation, Public Information, Public Order and Safety, Public Works and Highways, Tourism, Transportation, Basic Education and Culture, Ethics and Privileges, Human Rights, and Peace, Reconciliation and Unity.
  • Proposed the creation of a National Traffic Enforcement and Management Center to oversee and organize traffic enforcement nationwide.

Controversies

  • In March 2021, gained public attention through a viral video disputing a Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) traffic enforcer's decision to issue a ticket to a motorcycle passenger wearing slippers, leading to accusations of exploiting traffic enforcers for political gain.
  • Faced charges of grave coercion and usurpation of authority related to the 2021 incident, resulting in his arrest in February 2022; he posted bail for both charges.
