Danilo “Ka Daning” H. Ramos

Personal Information

Full Name: Danilo Hernandez Ramos

Date of Birth: September 27, 1956

Place of Birth: Malolos, Bulacan, Philippines

Age: 68

Political Party: Makabayang Koalisyon ng Mamamayan (Makabayan)

Key Advocacy Areas / Platforms

  • Genuine agrarian reform.
  • Food self-sufficiency.
  • Protection of farmers' rights.
  • Opposition to land conversion and the displacement of rural communities.

Notable Positions

  • Chairperson, Kilusang Magbubukid ng Pilipinas (KMP): Ramos leads the largest farmers' association in the Philippines, advocating for genuine agrarian reform and the rights of peasants.
  • Activism: With over 40 years of experience, Ramos has been at the forefront of the peasant movement, organizing protests, engaging in policy advocacy, and representing farmers' interests at various forums.

Controversies

  • Ramos has been a constant victim of state surveillance and red-tagging, facing harassment due to his activism.
