Rodante Marcoleta

Personal Information

Birthdate: July 29, 1953

Age on Election Day: 71

Birthplace: Paniqui, Tarlac

Residence: Brgy. San Isidro, Cainta, Rizal

Sex: Male

Civil Status: Married

Spouse: Edna M. Marcoleta

Profession/Occupation: Lawyer / Legislator

Political Party: SAGIP Party List

Platforms

  • Advocates for the welfare of the marginalized sectors, including the poor and vulnerable
  • Strong proponent of infrastructure and economic development
  • Focus on environmental sustainability and renewable energy initiatives

Notable Positions

  • Representative of SAGIP Party List (2016–present)

Senatorial Record

  • None (Marcoleta is a first-time senatorial candidate in 2025).

Notable Laws and Projects

  • Advocated for the National Land Use Act, which aims to strengthen land management in the country.
  • Supported the expansion of the National Anti-Drug Abuse Council (NAADAC) to help fight drug abuse at the grassroots level.

Controversies

  • Noted for his involvement in the controversial denial of ABS-CBN’s franchise renewal in 2020, where he was one of the principal advocates of the move to block it. His role in this issue garnered both support and backlash, with critics accusing him of being politically motivated in the effort.
