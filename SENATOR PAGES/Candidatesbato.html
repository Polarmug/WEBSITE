Ronald "Bato" dela Rosa

Personal Information

Full Name: Ronald Marapon Dela Rosa

Nickname: Bato

Birthdate: January 21, 1962

Age on Election Day: 63

Birthplace: Santa Cruz, Davao del Sur

Residence: Brgy. Magtuod, Davao City, Davao del Sur

Civil Status: Married to Grace Nancy Commandante Dela Rosa

Profession/Occupation: Government Official

Political Party: PDP–Laban

Political Platforms
